How it works

5 simple steps to start getting feedback from testers.

1

Register your app in your developer account.

2

Generate a link from your developer dashboard.

3

Share the link with people for testers to sign up.

4

Approve or reject the requests.

5

Testers can then give feedback to improve your app.
